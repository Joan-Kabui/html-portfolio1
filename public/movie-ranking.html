The Best Movies According to Joan

My top three movies of all-time.

How To Get Away with Murder

It shows how criminal law could be applied in real life cases.

Queen of The South

It outlines the way a nobody turned into a heffa. How drugs were smuggled through the borders of Mexico to the respective destinations and the challenges that they faced in the process of selling drugs.

The Rookie

The script writters try to show how the life of an individual called John Nolan changed his life at an advanced age. He tries to make changes in the police force and tries to do good in his career.
